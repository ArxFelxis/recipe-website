Shrimp Stir Fry Recipe

This shrimp stir fry recipe with frozen vegetables, frozen shrimp, and a simple stir fry sauce comes together in less than 30 minutes for a quick and easy dinner! Serve over rice or with noodles, and enjoy.

Shrimp stir fry is a quick, vibrant dish where tender shrimp are tossed with crisp vegetables in a hot pan. The high heat sears the shrimp just enough to stay juicy while locking in flavor. A savory sauce, often soy, based with garlic, ginger, or chiliâ€”coats everything, tying the seafood sweetness to the crunch of the vegetables. It is a fast, colorful meal that balances protein, texture, and bold flavor in each bite.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chicken stock
  • 1 tablespoon reduced-sodium soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste/li>
  • 3 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 1 (16 ounce) package frozen stir-fry vegetables
  • 20 uncooked medium shrimp, peeled and deveined

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Mix chicken stock, soy sauce, cornstarch, and garlic in a bowl; season with salt and pepper.
  3. Heat sesame oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat until oil shimmers; cook and stir vegetables in hot oil until softened, about 4 minutes.
  4. Add shrimp; cook and stir until shrimp begin to turn pink, about 3 minutes.
  5. Stir chicken stock mixture into vegetable-shrimp mixture. Continue to cook and stir until vegetables and shrimp are coated and sauce is thickened, about 5 minutes more.
